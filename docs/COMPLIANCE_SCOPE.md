# Compliance Scope

## Current Coverage
- ISPU-oriented pollutant interpretation with breakpoint logic.
- Compliance screening against selected PP No. 22/2021 ambient limits.
- Additional comparison layer using WHO 2021 guideline values.

## Important Limitation
Outputs in this prototype are screening indicators, not legal determinations.
